Top three activities to do in Eagan

See the latest films!

Emagine Eagan has all of the latest films and classic favorites. There's even axe throwing while you wait!

Go for a walk at the local park!

Lebanon Hills Regional Park is the perfect place for a picnic, going for a nice long walk, or just touching grass.

Visit Vikings Training Camp!

Come watch the Vikings train to do battle against their sworn nemeses: The Green Bay Packers!
